About the Sixmilebridge Historical Society

Sixmilebridge Historical Society was founded in 2020 to preserve the rich heritage of our community. Through our projects such as the commissioning of the Brendan O'Regan statue, historical plaques, heritage walks and public lectures, we are helping to keep our local history alive and accessible for future generations. Through research, storytelling, and community engagement, we celebrate our unique legacy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to research, preserve, and promote the rich history and heritage of Sixmilebridge and its environs for the benefit of the community and future generations. We aim to foster an appreciation for local history through talks, exhibitions, publications, and community engagement.
